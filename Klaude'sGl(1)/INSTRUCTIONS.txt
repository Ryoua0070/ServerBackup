--- INSTRUCTIONS: ---

1. Add the “[RP]Klaudes_Glyphs” resource pack to Minecraft and activate it in your world.
2. Go to this website: https://wiki.bedrock.dev/concepts/emojis.html
- Scroll to the bottom to find a guide and a converter.
3. Use the converter to get the glyph you want.
For example, for the glyph from glyph_E1.png in grid 02:
- Type “E102” in the HEX VALUE box.
- Click CONVERT to get your result.
4. Copy the result and paste it into your game chat. You should now see the glyphs!

--- FAQs: ---

[Q1] How do I use glyph_E8 or other glyphs?
Just repeat the process. Instead of using E1, use the number for the glyph you want. For glyph_E8, use E8. Your input should look like this: E800, E801, E802, etc. Then, click CONVERT.

[Q2] How do I add my own custom icons?
Modify the existing glyph PNG in the resource pack and add your icon in the next grids.
